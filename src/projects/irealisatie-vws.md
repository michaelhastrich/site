Everyone has the right to information about what the government does, how it does it, and why. Government bodies must provide that information on their own initiative, or when someone requests it. That information then becomes public.
I worked on the team that build the publishing platform and the public facing website where the documents after a WOO request are published. WCAG compliance and building a modular scalable frontend were our main responsibilities.
